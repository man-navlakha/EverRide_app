# EverRide — Project Information

> EverRide is a multimodal mobility platform that enables users to travel from their current location to their destination by combining different transport options such as walking, buses, and metro in a single journey.

> The platform plans seamless routes like walk → bus → metro → walk, allowing users to complete their trips efficiently through an integrated intercity travel system within one app.

## 1) Project Overview
- **Project Name:** EverRide
- **Main Idea / Vision:**
  - EverRide is a multimodal mobility platform designed to simplify urban travel by integrating multiple transportation options—such as walking, buses, and metro systems—into a single application. The platform helps users plan efficient routes from their current location to their destination by combining different transport modes in one seamless journey (e.g., walk → bus → metro → walk). The goal is to make intercity and urban travel faster, smarter, and more convenient.
- **Target Users:**
  - Daily commuters

Students and office workers

Tourists exploring the city

People who rely on public transportation

Users looking for the fastest and most cost‑effective routes
- **Primary Platforms:**
  -Mobile App: Android & iOS (built with React Native)

Backend API: Node.js / Express

Admin Panel: Web dashboard for managing routes, data, and analytics
- **Current Stage:**
  - MVP
